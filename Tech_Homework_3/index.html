My Five Year Plan

Here are my plans until 2025.

For the rest of 2020, I plan to live lowkey, save money, sell things online, and spend my free time doing school work and doing well in my internship.

For the first half of 2021, I will continue that trend and graduate in May. I will also sell almost all of my possessions. In June, I plan to board a plane to Bangkok, Thailand with my girlfriend.

For the second half of 2021, I will explore the land of my ancestors and live relatively workless. I will spend 3 months in Koh Samui, Thailand before using the next 3 months to find a permanent condo to sign a yearly lease and begin looking for work.

For the first half of 2022, I will continue to look for work from Bangkok. I will save money on cost of living, and have a purpose for doing so.

For the second half of 2022, I will keep working and saving money while enjoying the wonderful world of Thailand.

For the first half of 2023, I will continue living in Thailand and begin exploring moving around to other places of the world for short vacations.

For the second half of 2023, this will continue :)

For the first half of 2024, this will continue :)

For the second half of 2024, I will consider coming home to the USA to buy land and have a home. I will look into buying property in Pennsylvania or Colorado (or both!)

For the first half og 2025, I will come back home to the states and live out my life on a small farm that is self-sufficient, efficient, and productive.
